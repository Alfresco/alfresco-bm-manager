To create an archetype:

> mvn clean install

> mvn archetype:create-from-project
> cd target\generated-sources\archetype

Bring the archetype's pom in
> del pom.xml
> copy ..\..\..\archetype-pom.xml pom.xml

Remove unecessary files:
> del src\main\resources\archetype-resources\archetype-*.xml
> del src\main\resources\archetype-resources\building-the-archetype.txt
> del src\main\resources\archetype-resources\*.log
> del src\main\resources\archetype-resources\*.log.*
> del src\main\resources\archetype-resources\.project
> del src\main\resources\archetype-resources\.classpath
> rmdir /S src\main\resources\archetype-resources\bin
> rmdir /S src\main\resources\archetype-resources\.settings

Check and deploy archetype artifact:
> mvn clean install
> mvn deploy
